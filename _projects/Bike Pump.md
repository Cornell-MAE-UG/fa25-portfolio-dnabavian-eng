For class we were asked to take apart an assigned fluid mechanical system, in this case a bike pump and analyze it. My contribution to this was leading the dissection, and finding the data as seen in the video. I also organized meetings to work on the project. In the video I helped explain how this bike pump works as a positive displacement pump and the fluid mechanics behind each part to make the pump work as it does. I also reviewed all of my partners' content, checking over the math and cohesiveness of the presentation. Overall this was a great project for communication skills, collaboration, and a fun side of fluid mechanics.
